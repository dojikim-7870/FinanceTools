Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find your answer below:

Are your calculators accurate?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are designed to provide accurate estimates. However, they are for educational purposes only and should not replace professional financial advice. Actual results may vary based on specific terms, conditions, and individual circumstances.

Do you store my financial data?

No, we do not store your personal financial information. All calculations are performed locally in your web browser, and your data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Is FinanceTools.info really free?

Yes, all our calculators and educational content are completely free to use. We support our service through advertising partnerships, but we never charge users for access to our tools.

Can I use these calculators for commercial purposes?

Our calculators are designed for personal use and professional reference. If you're a financial advisor or professional, you may reference our tools, but please ensure you provide appropriate disclaimers to your clients. See our Terms of Service for full details.

How often do you update your calculators?

We regularly review and update our calculators to reflect changes in tax laws, interest rates, and financial regulations. Major updates are implemented as needed, and we continuously monitor for accuracy and functionality improvements.

Do you provide personalized financial advice?

No, we do not provide personalized financial advice. Our tools and content are for educational purposes only. For specific financial advice tailored to your situation, please consult with a qualified financial advisor, tax professional, or other appropriate specialist.

Can I suggest new calculator features?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new calculators and features. Please use the contact form above with the subject "Feature Request" and provide details about what you'd like to see added to our platform.

What browsers do you support?

Our calculators work on all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience. Our site is also optimized for mobile devices.

Technical Support

Experiencing Technical Issues?

If you're experiencing problems with our calculators or website, here are some troubleshooting steps you can try:

Common Solutions

  • Refresh the page: Press F5 or Ctrl+R (Cmd+R on Mac) to reload the page
  • Clear browser cache: Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again
  • Disable extensions: Temporarily disable browser extensions/add-ons that might interfere
  • Try a different browser: Test the calculator in another browser
  • Check your connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Enable JavaScript: Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings

When Reporting Issues

To help us resolve technical issues quickly, please include the following information:

  • Your operating system (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android)
  • Browser name and version
  • Specific calculator or page where the issue occurs
  • Description of what happened vs. what you expected
  • Any error messages you received
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
